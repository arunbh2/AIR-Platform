About Practical Action

The AIR Platform is a Practical Action initiative designed to accelerate climate resilience across Asia.

Pioneering Climate Resilience Across Asia

Practical Action is an international development organization that puts ingenious ideas to work so people in poverty can change their world. We help people find solutions to some of the world's toughest problems, including climate change and environmental degradation.

The AIR (Augmented Intelligence for Resilience) Platform represents Practical Action's innovative approach to building climate resilience in communities across Asia by integrating technology, collaborative knowledge sharing, and crowdfunding-inspired funding mechanisms.

Through the AIR Platform, Practical Action is creating a comprehensive ecosystem to support climate adaptation and resilience-building initiatives, connecting communities, experts, and resources to create lasting impact.
